Privacy Policy

At Vincent Borje Retail Hub, your privacy matters to us. We are committed to keeping your personal information secure and using it responsibly to enhance your shopping experience. This policy describes how we collect, use, protect, and share your data as you interact with our store and website.

What Information We Collect

We may collect details such as your name, contact information, delivery address, order history, and payment preferences when you shop with us or contact our team.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your orders and deliver products safely to you
  • To communicate updates about your purchases, delivery, and promotions
  • To personalize your experience and respond to your requests
  • To improve our products, services, and website functionality

How We Protect Your Data

Your information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized staff. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to other businesses. Security measures are in place to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your information.

Sharing Information

We may share necessary information with trusted partners, such as payment processors or delivery services, solely to fulfill your order and provide services. These partners are required to protect your data and use it only as needed for our business.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You may request updates, corrections, or removal of your personal information by contacting us
  • You can choose to opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • If you have questions about how your data is handled, reach out to our team for more details

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.

If you have any concerns or inquiries regarding your privacy, please email us at contact@vincentborjeretail.com or call +63 945 824 7103. We value your trust and are here to help.
